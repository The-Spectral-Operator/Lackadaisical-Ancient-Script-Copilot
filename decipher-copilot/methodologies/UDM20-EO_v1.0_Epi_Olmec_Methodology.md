## Core Position

UDM20-EO targets the **Epi-Olmec** (Isthmian) script (c. 500 BCE – 500 CE) from the Gulf Coast of Mesoamerica. A partial decipherment was proposed (Justeson & Kaufman 1993) reading the script as pre-proto-Zoquean, but this remains contested. The script is logosyllabic (like Maya) and likely encodes a Mixe-Zoquean language.
